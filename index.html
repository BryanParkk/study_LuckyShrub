Get plant care pro tips

Watering Wisdom

Different plants have different watering needs. Check soil moisture before watering and adjust frequency based on plant type and season.

Lighting Essentials

Understand your plant's light requirements. Place sun-loving plants in bright spots and shade-tolerant ones in low-light areas for optimal growth.

Soil Savvy

Use well-draining soil suited for your plants. Regularly check for compacted soil, and repot if needed to ensure healthy root growth and proper nutrient absorption.

Greetings

Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm based in Tuscon, Arizona, dedicated to transforming spaces into stunning natural havens. Founded by Jason and Maria, both passionate about plants, Lucky Shrub offers comprehensive services in garden design, creation, maintenance, landscaping, and operates a small plant nursery.

Jason, the garden architect, brings years of experience in designing harmonious outdoor spaces. His meticulous approach ensures that each design reflects the client's vision while harmonizing with nature. Maria oversees marketing and nursery operations, ensuring a wide range of high-quality plants for clients to choose from.

At Lucky Shrub, we believe in the therapeutic power of gardens and their ability to enhance well-being. Whether creating a tranquil backyard retreat, a vibrant garden oasis, or a functional urban space, our team works closely with clients to bring their ideas to life. We prioritize sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials and native plants to promote biodiversity and conserve resources.

Our portfolio showcases diverse projects, from residential gardens to commercial landscapes, each showcasing our commitment to excellence and creativity. Our client-centric approach focuses on understanding individual needs, budget considerations, and long-term maintenance, ensuring lasting beauty and functionality.

Join us at Lucky Shrub to experience the joy of nature-inspired living spaces, where every garden tells a unique story of beauty, serenity, and sustainability.
